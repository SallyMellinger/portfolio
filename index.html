Hi. I'm Sally.

A few months ago, I quit my job to pursue my dream of learning web development.

I was working for Tinuiti, one of the largest media + digital agencies in North America as their Content Operations Manager. It was a dream job with perks to-die-for, but I couldn't shake the feeling that I needed to learn web development. It had been an interest of mine since I was a kid, and dabbling in Wordpress HTML and CSS to fix minor website issues at my job just wasn't cutting it.

To rid myself the possibility of a lifetime of 'what-ifs,' I decided to take the leap and enroll at Eleven Fifty Academy in Fishers, Indiana.

Since jumping ship and diving head first into web development, I'm realizing web development complements my digital marketing and content writing skills perfectly. That is to say, there is magic in both writing the content and then having the ability to bring those words to life on a webpage that I've designed and developed with marketing and SEO best-practices in mind.

I can't wait to finish up my web development certification at Eleven Fifty Academy and bring my content and digital marketing expertise and web development skill set to my next opportunity.
